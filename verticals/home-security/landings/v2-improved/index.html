Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free equipment offer?

The free equipment typically includes a control panel, door/window sensors, motion detectors, and at least one HD security camera — depending on your qualifying plan. A specialist will confirm exactly what's included for your home.

How long does professional installation take?

Most installations are completed in under 2 hours. Our installers handle everything — equipment placement, wiring, app setup, and a full walkthrough so you know exactly how your system works.

Is there a contract required?

Some plans include a service agreement (typically 36 months) which is what allows free equipment and installation. Shorter and no-contract options are also available. A specialist will walk you through all options — no pressure.

What does 24/7 professional monitoring actually mean?

It means real trained professionals — not automated systems — monitoring your sensors around the clock. If an alarm triggers, they verify the threat and contact emergency services on your behalf, even if you can't be reached.

Can I add more devices later?

Yes. All systems are fully expandable. Start with basic coverage and add cameras, smart locks, garage sensors, or a thermostat at any time — no reinstall required.
