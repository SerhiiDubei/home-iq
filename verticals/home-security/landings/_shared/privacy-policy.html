Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

1. Who We Are

Home IQ is a referral marketplace connecting US homeowners with licensed local home-security installers. We do not install systems or perform monitoring ourselves.

2. Information We Collect

  • You provide: ZIP code, name, phone number, email, and the home improvement project you're interested in.
  • Automatic: IP address (for location matching), browser type, pages visited, referrer URL.
  • Cookies and pixels: Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, and conversion tracking for advertising attribution.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Match you with up to 4 licensed contractors in your area.
  • Send your quote request via email/SMS/phone to those contractors.
  • Measure ad performance and improve the site.
  • Send you offers and follow-ups (you can unsubscribe at any time).

4. Who We Share It With

Only the contractors you request quotes from, plus our analytics/marketing service providers (Google, Meta, Sentry). We never sell your personal data to third parties for unrelated marketing.

5. TCPA Consent

By submitting our form, you agree to be contacted by phone, SMS, or email (including pre-recorded messages and autodialed calls) at the number you provide, even if it's on a do-not-call list. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

6. Your Rights (CCPA / GDPR)

You can request to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. Email us at privacy@homeimprove.io with your request.

7. Children

Our service is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy: privacy@homeimprove.io
