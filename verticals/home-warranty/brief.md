# Home Warranty — Brief

## Overview
Home warranty plans covering major systems and appliances. Annual subscription service.

## Target Audience
- Age range: 30–65
- Homeowner type: new homeowners, owners of older homes/appliances
- Pain point: fear of unexpected repair costs, aging appliances and systems
- Motivation to act: recent expensive repair, new home purchase, budgeting for repairs
