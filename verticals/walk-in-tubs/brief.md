## Overview

- Age range: 55–80
- Homeowner type: seniors, aging-in-place, caregivers purchasing for parents
- Pain point: fall risk in standard tub, limited mobility, joint pain
- Motivation to act: fall incident, doctor recommendation, aging parent moving in
