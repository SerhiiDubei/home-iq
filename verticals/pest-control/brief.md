# Pest Control — Brief

## Overview
Residential pest control services: extermination, prevention plans, and recurring treatment subscriptions.

## Target Audience
- Age range: 28–60
- Homeowner type: all homeowners, especially in warmer climates and older homes
- Pain point: active infestation (ants, roaches, rodents, termites, bed bugs), fear of damage
- Motivation to act: visible pests, seasonal surge, moving into new home, property damage concern
